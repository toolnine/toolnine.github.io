About OneTool

OneTool is a lightweight, privacy-first collection of online tools for everyday tasks, developers, and creators.

We believe that online tools should be fast, reliable, and respectful of user privacy. That's why every single tool on OneTool runs entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to our servers for processing, ensuring that your data remains private and secure on your device.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive toolkit where you can find everything you need without searching through different websites. From image compression to text formatting and document scanning, OneTool aims to simplify your workflow.

Why Choose OneTool?

Privacy First

No data leaves your device. We don't save or process your files on our servers.

100% Free

All tools are completely free to use without any limitations or hidden charges.

Fast Performance

Because everything runs locally, our tools work significantly faster than server-based alternatives.
