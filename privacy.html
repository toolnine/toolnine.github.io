Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data on OneTool.

OneTool is designed as a privacy-first application. All data processing (such as image compression, PDF creation, and text encryption) occurs directly on your device using your browser's capabilities. We do not transmit, store, or process any of your personal files or input data on our servers.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage (a feature of your web browser) to save basic preferences to enhance your experience. This includes saving your selected theme mode (light or dark) and other non-identifying settings. These settings are stored locally on your device only.

  • **Functional Cookies:** We use strictly necessary cookies/local storage for essential features, such as remembering your theme preference.

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us
