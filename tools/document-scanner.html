How to Use

  1. Click "Start Camera" to capture documents using your device's camera.
  2. While the camera is on, apply live filters like "Black & White" or "Sharpen" to improve image clarity.
  3. For each page, click "Capture Page" or add an image file.
  4. Click "Generate PDF" to create a single PDF containing all captured pages.
  5. Download or share the final document.
