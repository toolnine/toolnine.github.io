How to Use

  1. Click on the box or drag and drop your image file.
  2. (New) Use the "Keep Aspect Ratio" option to maintain proportion when changing dimensions.
  3. Enter the new width and/or height. If aspect ratio lock is enabled, changing one value will automatically update the other.
  4. Click "Resize & Download" to process the image.
  5. The resized image will appear for preview and download.
