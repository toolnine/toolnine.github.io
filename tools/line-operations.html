Sort, shuffle, or remove duplicate lines from your text input. All processing is private and happens in your browser.

How to Use

  1. Paste your list of items or lines of text into the input box above.
  2. Choose an operation to perform: sort alphabetically, reverse sort, shuffle randomly, or remove identical lines.
  3. (New) Use "Trim Lines" to remove extra spaces from the start and end of each line.
  4. The result will appear in the output box.
  5. Use the copy button to get the processed text or "Swap Input/Output" to continue editing.
