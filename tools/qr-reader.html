How to Use

  1. Click "Start Camera Scanner" to enable your device camera for real-time scanning. Point the camera at a QR code or barcode.
  2. Alternatively, click "Upload Image File" to select a photo containing a QR code or barcode from your device.
  3. Scan results will appear in the "Scanned Data" section below.
  4. Use the options to copy individual codes or export all results to CSV/JSON.
