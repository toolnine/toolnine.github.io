Encrypt or decrypt text messages locally in your browser using a secure AES-GCM algorithm with a custom password.

How to Use

  1. Enter the text you want to encrypt in the top box.
  2. Enter a strong passphrase in the password field.
  3. Click "Encrypt" to generate the encrypted text.
  4. To decrypt, paste the encrypted text into the top box, re-enter the original passphrase, and click "Decrypt".
